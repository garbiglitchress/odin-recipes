Oatmeal

Description

Oatmeal is a versatile nutritous food that can be made many different ways, depending on your mood. (Why am I prescribing a recipe then? I dunno ¯\_(ツ)_/¯)

Ingredients

  • Oats
  • Milk
  • Peanut Butter
  • Whey Protein
  • Cinnamon

Steps

  1. Put Oats and Milk in saucepan
  2. Put saucepan on stove and wait on it to heat up
  3. After it heats up and the oats are cooking, stir occasionally, adding PB
  4. After its texture is good, turn off oven and add protein and cinnamon
  5. Enjoy! (The most crucial step ;))
