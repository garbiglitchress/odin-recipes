Chips

Description

Chips are nice when you've had a long day and just want something salty and savory to unwind with. Follow this recipe to maximize your chip-eating experience!

Ingredients

  • Chips
  • Bowl
  • Beverage

Steps

  1. Get out a bowl.
  2. Put the chips inside.
  3. Enjoy, and be sure to wash down with your beverage of choice!
